NIL Compliance Based on you

NIL compliance, which stands for "Name, Image, and Likeness," refers to a set of rules and regulations that govern how college athletes can benefit from their own names, images, and likenesses while maintaining their eligibility to compete in collegiate sports. Prior to NIL changes, NCAA (National Collegiate Athletic Association) rules strictly prohibited college athletes from profiting from their own NIL rights.
