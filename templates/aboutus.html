About Us

Before 2021, college athletes in the United States faced significant challenges when it came to securing financial support or finding sponsorships. The stringent NIL (Name, Image, and Likeness) restrictions imposed by the NCAA (National Collegiate Athletic Association) prevented them from benefiting financially from their talent and reputation. However, with recent changes in legislation across many states, college athletes now have the opportunity to receive compensation from their supporters and seek sponsorships to enhance their personal brands. Despite these positive legal developments, our research revealed a critical gap in the market. There was no dedicated platform to assist college athletes in promoting themselves and establishing valuable sponsorship connections. While well-funded companies could easily identify and sponsor talented athletes, the same opportunities were not readily available to individual athletes. This is where SponsorIn comes in.

We are a web application designed to empower college athletes and companies alike. SponsorIn serves as a connecting bridge, facilitating the collaboration between talented athletes and businesses looking to invest in their potential. Our platform creates an environment for athletes and companies to network, maintain meaningful connections, and foster mutually beneficial business relationships. With SponsorIn, college athletes gain the ability to proactively showcase their skills and seek sponsorship opportunities, leveling the playing field. Meanwhile, companies can identify athletes with outstanding potential and support them in growing their personal brands under a sponsorship arrangement. We are here to transform the landscape for college athletes, making it easier than ever for them to realize their full potential and secure the support they deserve.
